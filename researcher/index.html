Early Childhood Care and Education Data Dashboard

Data Summary

The ECCE_Data_2022.csv file contains data related to Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE) programs across various districts in Bihar, India. The dataset includes detailed information about the programs, their timings, the children enrolled, the facilities available, and the teachers involved. Below is a summary of the key columns and the type of information they contain:

  • S.No: Serial number of the record.
  • Unique Code: A unique identifier for each record.
  • Name of the State: The name of the state (e.g., Bihar).
  • State Code: A code representing the state.
  • Name of the District: The name of the district within the state.
  • Name of the Programme: The name of the ECCE program.
  • Address of the Programme: The address of the program, including landmarks.
  • Type of Programme: The type of ECCE program.
  • Timing of the Programme (as per government): The official start and end times of the program.
  • Timing of the Programme (as per centre): The actual start and end times of the program at the center.
  • Medium of Instruction: The language used for instruction.
  • Age Score of the Children: The age score of the children enrolled.
  • Number of Children Enrolled: The number of children enrolled in the grade assessed.
  • Number of Children Present: The number of children present, both as marked in the register and as a headcount.
  • Incharge of the Class: The person in charge of the class or grade assessed.
  • Name of the Administrator: The name of the administrator of the program.
  • Date of Administration: The date when the program was administered.
  • Time of Administration: The start and end times of the administration.
  • Verified by: The person who verified the data.
  • Number of Activities Observed: The number of activities observed during the program.
  • Various Activities and Facilities: Columns detailing various activities (e.g., group activity, storytelling, rhymes) and facilities (e.g., playground, clean water, hygiene practices) available at the center.
  • Teacher Information: Columns detailing information about the teachers, including their name, gender, age, type of teacher, total teaching experience, educational qualifications, and training received.

  • The dataset includes detailed information about the timing, activities, and facilities of each ECCE program.
  • It also provides comprehensive data about the teachers, including their qualifications and training.
  • The dataset is useful for analyzing the quality and effectiveness of ECCE programs in Bihar.
