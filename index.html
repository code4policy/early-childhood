This data is collected and managed by UNICEF India as part of their partnership with Anganwadi Centers. Each year, UNICEF data collectors, alongside government monitoring officers, visit each site to conduct observations. Together, they engage in a norming process to agree on a final score for each site based on predefined criteria. This scoring system allows UNICEF to strategically prioritize support and allocate resources to centers most in need.
